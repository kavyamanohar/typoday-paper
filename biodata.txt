Santhosh Thottingal is a language computing professional and typeface designer. He works at Language Engineering, Wikimedia Foundation. He has been actively working on Malayalam language computing with Swathanthra Malayalam Computing(SMC), a non-profit organization, building critical language computing infrastructure for Malayalam. Santhosh designed two very popular typefaces Chilanka and Manjari. He also co-maintains about a dozen of widely used Malayalam fonts by SMC.
Kavya Manohar is language computing passionate. She is Assistant Professor, Government Engineering college, Palakkad. Kavya co-maintains Malayalam fonts by SMC focusing on opentype engineering.
